Marsha Blackburn

Brief Bio

Blackburn is the representative for Tennessee’s 7th congressional district. She has served since Jan 7, 2003. Blackburn is next up for reelection in 2018.

Political Affiliation

Blackburn is a member of the Republican party.

Committees

Marsha Blackburn sits on the following committees:

  • House Committee on Energy and Commerce
  • Member: Subcommittee on Communications and Technology
  • Member: Subcommittee on Environment
  • Member: Subcommittee on Health
